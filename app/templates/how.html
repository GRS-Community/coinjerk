To get groestltip donations on your stream you need to do the following things:

1
set up your streaming system to receive streamlabs widget alerts. A guide for OBS and Xsplit can be found here: Adding widgets to OBS/Xsplit
2
setup your tip page. Link your groestlcoin wallet to the groestltip system here by using a Master Public Key (xpub). This does not let anyone have access to your wallet, it only let us get unused addresses from your wallet. It is read only. A guide to setting up the system using the android wallet can be found here.
This will work on ios on the groestlpay wallet too! You can also add your PayPal email to receive PayPal donations too. Now you're ready to go! Simply share your groestltip address and let people donate.
