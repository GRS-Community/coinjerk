To get groestltip donations displayed on your stream you need to do the following things:

1
set up your streaming system to receive streamlabs widget alerts. A guide for OBS and Xsplit can be found here: Adding widgets to OBS/Xsplit
2
setup your tip page (it is made on first login). Link your groestlcoin wallet to the groestltip system here by using a Master Public Key (xpub). This does not let anyone have access to your wallet, it only let us get unused addresses from your wallet. It is read only. A guide to setting up the system using the android wallet can be found here.
This will work in the iOS version!
3
If you want, you can also accept Paypal donations! Add your paypal email here! (must be logged in)
4
Now you're ready to go! Simply share your groestltip page ( groestltip.groestlcoin.org/user/username ) and let people donate!
